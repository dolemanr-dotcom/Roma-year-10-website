England

A country located just off Europe, England is dotted with villages and big cities, with one of the most iconic cities, London.

Places worth visiting:

  • Stonehenge - an ancient landmark believed to have been created in the Stone age, Stonehenge is a formation of large rocks, and is great for people who are interested in historical sites.

Stonehenge - Sumit Surai - Wikimedia Commons - CC BY SA

  • The Shard - the tallest skyscraper in London, this building is made entirely of glass and overlooks the city.

The Shard - Colin - Wikimedia Commons - CC BY SA

  • Kew Gardens - a huge botanic garden in London, this landmark is great at any time of the year. At Christmas, Kew Gardens is well-known for its amazing light shows.

Kew Gardens - Diliff - Wikimedia Commons - CC BY SA

Popular Food:

  • Sunday Roast - a big lunch made up of a protein (pork, beef, or chicken), yorkshire pudding, gravy, potatoes, and carrots.

Sunday Roast - The Stag Restaurant - Wikimedia Commons - CC BY SA

  • Fish and chips - created in England in the 19th century, fish and chips is a staple at an English pub.

Fish and Chips - Learning Lark - Wikimedia Commons - CC BY
