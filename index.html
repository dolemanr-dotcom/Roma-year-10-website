This is a helpful website that you can use to plan a holiday! It will tell you about different countries - which food they eat and what landmarks are worth visiting!

Germany - A country located in central Europe, perfect for a flower-filled spring or a traditional, festive Christmas

Singapore - A small country in South-East Asia, the list never runs out for things to do and it is an amazing tropical retreat

England - a country near Europe, England is part of the United Kingdom, and is a mixture of historical and modern

California - a state on the west coast of America, California is a huge place, full of cities, and warm all-year round

Rarotonga - located in the Pacific Ocean, Rarotonga is a lovely island filled with welcoming and cheerful people
